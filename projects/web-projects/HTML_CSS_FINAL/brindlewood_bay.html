About Brindlewood Bay

Brindlewood Bay is a unique and narrative-focused tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) created by Jason Cordova. It blends cozy mystery vibes with cosmic horror, drawing inspiration from shows like Murder, She Wrote and the works of H.P. Lovecraft. Players take on the roles of elderly women in a small coastal town, solving murder mysteries while uncovering a dark, supernatural conspiracy.
