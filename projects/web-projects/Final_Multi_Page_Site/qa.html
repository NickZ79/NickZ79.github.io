My Role at Sagenverse

Sagenverse is a small software development company based in Ann Arbor, Michigan. I’ve been working as a QA (Quality Assurance) Analyst there for about two years. My job is to help ensure the software we build works as expected. I test new features, look for bugs, and work closely with our developers to make sure we catch and fix issues before they reach users.

What is Sagenverse?

The team at Sagenverse is made up of creative and curious people who care about storytelling and technology. We're building tools that help people create and share stories in new, interactive ways. It's about using tech to bring traditional storytelling into a modern, immersive space—making it more accessible and dynamic for everyone.
